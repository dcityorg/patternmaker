# Using PatternMaker with VaseMaker

PatternMaker and [VaseMaker](https://vasemaker.dcity.org) are companion apps. You can design a seamless pattern in PatternMaker and apply it as a surface texture on a 3D-printable vase.

## Workflow

1. **Design your pattern** in PatternMaker
2. **Export SVG** (File → Export SVG, or the Export SVG button)
3. **Open VaseMaker** at [vasemaker.dcity.org](https://vasemaker.dcity.org)
4. In the **Textures** section, enable **SVG Pattern**
5. Click **Load SVG** and select your exported file — or click **Paste SVG** and paste the SVG code
6. Adjust **# Tiles Around** to set how many times the pattern wraps around the vase
7. Adjust **# Tiles Vert** to control the vertical repeat
8. Use **Depth** to control how deep the pattern displaces the surface

## Tips

- **High contrast works best** — pure black and white patterns give crisp relief texture
- **Grayscale SVGs** produce multi-depth effects (darker = deeper groove) — this can be intentional and beautiful
- For **cutout / lattice vases**, enable the **Cutout** toggle in VaseMaker's SVG Pattern section — dark areas become holes through the wall
- Use **Smooth Zones** in VaseMaker (base and rim %) to keep the top and bottom of the vase solid even with a cutout pattern
- Increase VaseMaker's **radial resolution** (200–360+) for finer pattern detail on the vase surface
